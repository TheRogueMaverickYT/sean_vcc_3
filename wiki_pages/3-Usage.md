# Usage

Operating the Turbo-Powered Skateboard requires immense focus and a lack of self-preservation.

1. Turn on the board using the master switch.
2. Pair the handheld remote via Bluetooth.
3. Step onto the board.
4. Slowly engage the throttle. DO NOT slam it unless you want to do a backflip.
