# Turbo-Powered Skateboards 🛹💥

Welcome to the future of personal transport! The Turbo-Powered Skateboard is an open-source hardware and software project dedicated to strapping literal micro-jet engines and high-torque electric motors to a plank of wood. 

Whether you're looking to commute at Mach 0.5 or just want to terrify your local neighborhood, you've come to the right place.

Please check out our Wiki for detailed information on how to build, install, and survive your first ride.
